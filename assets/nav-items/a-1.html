Aleksandar Paunković

Aleksandar Paunković was born on March 11, 1994 in Prokuplje. He graduated from the High School of Electrical Engineering, Department of Electrical Automation in 2013. He enrolled at the Faculty of Applied Arts in Belgrade in 2014. He graduated in 2018 in the study program of applied art, module of applied painting, in the class of professor Miroslav Lazović. Master's degree in 2019 at the Faculty of Applied Arts in Belgrade, module applied painting.
