This is our gallery plan.

The gallery space consists of a Great and a Small gallery, a wardrobe, a warehouse, a depot, a kitchen and a toilet. The Great Gallery is 14 meters long and 12 meters wide. There are two pillars in the middle. Exhibition panels have reflectors whose position is adjustable.The Small Gallery measures 5 meters and 7 meters. The height of both the Great Gallery and the Small Gallery is 3.5 meters. Both galleries have separate entrances. In the small Gallery there is a permanent exhibition, while in the large there is a guest exhibition. Neon bulbs were built on the ceiling. The gallery is equipped with air conditioning and ventilation.
