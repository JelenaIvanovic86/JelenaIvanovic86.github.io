Milena Andric-Artist

Milena Andric was born on 02.08.1979 . in Kragujevac/Serbia. She graduated on Faculty of Applied Arts and Design in Belgrade/Serbia, section- textile design .She works as a full-time art teacher since 2004 in Kragujevac/Serbia . She specializes in oil paintings and also in unique handpainted textile and leather artefactes. Since 2010 she works as a court expert for fine and applied art. She is responsible for the selection of artists and there work.

Kristijan Klačar-Lights & Music

Kristijan Klačar was born on 03.07.1972 . in Kragujevac. He graduated on Faculty of Electronic and Design in Belgrade/Serbia . He works as a full-time tehnical support for the music events, and is responsible for the lighting and sound for our exhibitions.
