Milan Pantelić

Milan Pantelić is an artist, art theoretician and co-founder of Neomodernism, an art movement that espouses spiritual and aesthetic values in art. His work offers a clear vision that mirrors universal aspects of life. The world that Milan presents is as convincing as it is mysterious. Drawing on his Sufi background, Milan has embodied in his art the ancient mystical beliefs in inner spirituality. Milan’s artistic output has been attracting many art lovers and collectors across Europe and the USA for the past ten years. Clear in his concepts, confident in the expression of his idea, but also unimposing in his message, Milan appears as a mature artist whose pictures stand out from the work of other abstract artists. The harmony of form and colour created in his pictures often correlates to the harmony in classical music compositions. In this sense Milan is an established conductor orchestrating a play of relations between colour, depth and composition. In his work Milan always strives towards achieving a balance between the subjective and the objective. If abstract paintings are born out of figurative ones then in Milan’s work these two aspects of human duality approach one another in a mutual balance and unity.
